About TEDx Kiwanis Park Youth


At TEDx Kiwanis Park Youth, we understand that curiosity isn't just a trait, it's the fuel that drives us towards groundbreaking discoveries. Our event will showcase a diverse array of speakers from various disciplines—each chosen for their ability to inspire, challenge, and provoke thought among our attendees. Through their stories, insights, and experiences, we aim to illuminate the ways in which curiosity can lead as a powerful force for change.

What is TEDx


TEDx events are independently organized, community-based gatherings designed to share a TED-like experience. They are run by local volunteers who obtain a license from TED, allowing them to create events that feature TED Talk videos and live speakers to spark discussion and connection. These events adhere to the guidelines and spirit of TED's mission of "ideas worth spreading" but are tailored to the local community's interests and needs. Unlike TED Conferences, which are organized directly by TED, TEDx events are self-organized, promoting local engagement and idea-sharing on a global scale​
